Visit Mallorca

Mallorca is a small island in the Balearic Islands of Spain. It is extremely historical with a lot of amazing architecture and crystal-clear beaches. The island has two spoken languages, Spanish and Catalan. Most locals are able to speak both, and children also have classes in school.

Palma de Mallorca

The main city in Mallorca is called Palma. About 400,000 people live in this city. It is home to a lot of history and shopping. It also has its own beach!

Alcúdia

Alcúdia is on the opposite side of the island of Mallorca from Palma. It is less populated and is a popular vacation spot. It has a lot of nature for hiking and is a popular spot for boats to dock. Alcúdia also has a cathedral that dates back to 1302!

Traditional Mallorquin Food

Mallorca has a lot of great food but my favorites are sobrassada, which is a spreadable cold cut, ensaimada, which is a pastry with lard, and paella con pollo.
